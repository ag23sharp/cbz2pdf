**Configuration:**

* **Locate:** Enabled for macOS, with a delay of 120 minutes.
* **SSH:** Enabled for macOS, scheduled to turn on at 18:00 every day.
* **Mail:** Not enabled.

**Explanation:**

This configuration ensures that:

1. **Locate:** If the macOS device is lost or stolen, it can be located using the Find My Device service, even if the battery is low. However, there is a 2-hour delay before the device's location can be determined.
2. **SSH:** SSH access to the macOS device will be enabled at 18:00 every day, allowing remote connection for maintenance or troubleshooting purposes.
3. **Mail:** Mail access is not enabled, as it is not specified in the configuration provided.
