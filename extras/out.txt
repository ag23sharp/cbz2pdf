* **Reliability:** Nokia phones are also known for their reliability. They are often updated with the latest software and security patches, and they typically have a long lifespan.
* **User-friendliness:** Nokia phones are designed to be easy to use, even for first-time smartphone users. They have a simple and intuitive interface, and they come with a variety of pre-installed apps and features.
* **Value for money:** Nokia phones are typically priced competitively, making them a great value for money. They offer a lot of features and functionality for a relatively low price.
